Who We Are

About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation is a youth-driven, government-registered non-profit organization dedicated to creating positive social impact across India. Registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act 2013, our mission is to address grassroots social and environmental issues through sustainable community welfare programs.

Driven by compassion and youth leadership, we feed stray animals, educate underprivileged kids, support daily wage earners, plant trees, empower women, and offer skill development workshops.

Our Programs

Ongoing Projects

We work systematically across six core focus areas to support communities in need.

Project SEVA

Addressing food insecurity by distributing fresh meals, dry rations, and clothing to underprivileged communities and daily wage earners.

Project BACHPANSHALA

Providing high-quality educational resources, academic mentoring, and study tools to shape the futures of underprivileged children.

Project PRAKRITI

Promoting environmental conservation through community tree-planting drives, sanitation awareness, and cleanups.

Project UDAAN

Empowering women with vocational training, computer literacy classes, and skill development workshops to achieve independence.

Project JEEV

Fostering kindness by rescuing and feeding street animals, organizing vaccination drives, and promoting animal care.

Project VIKAS

Guiding and nurturing youth career prospects by offering skill-based professional internships and leadership modules.

Our Purpose

Why Our Work Matters

We target key areas to ensure sustainable, long-term social growth.

Hunger Relief

Providing basic nutrition so that daily wage earners and families can focus on building a stable future without starvation.

Education Access

Giving children study materials and tutoring classes to ensure school dropouts are minimized.

Conservation

Planting trees and conducting cleanups to establish clean air and sustainable local environments.

Women Livelihoods

Helping local women acquire professional skills like tailoring to earn an active family income.

Animal Support

Feeding and treating stray dogs and domestic animals to maintain a safe, disease-free neighborhood.

Youth Leadership

Training students through real-life internships to make them skilled, responsible civic leaders.
